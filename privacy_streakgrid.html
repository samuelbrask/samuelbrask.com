Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

🔒 StreakGrid does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who we are

StreakGrid is developed and published by Brask Digital (CVR: 46328019), owned by Samuel Brask Marosi. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at samuel@samuelbrask.com.

2. Data we collect

StreakGrid stores your habit data — including habit names, icons, completion history, and reminder settings — locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

StreakGrid also stores your personal preferences locally, including your chosen habits, notification times, and subscription status. This data never leaves your device.

3. Notifications

StreakGrid can send you local push notifications as habit reminders. These are generated entirely on your device and are not connected to any external server. You can enable or disable notifications at any time via Settings → Notifications → StreakGrid.

4. Widgets

StreakGrid offers a home screen widget that displays your habit progress. Widget data is shared between the app and the widget extension using Apple's App Groups feature, entirely on your device. No data is sent externally.

5. Subscriptions

StreakGrid offers auto-renewable subscriptions:

  • Weekly: $0.99/week
  • Monthly: $1.99/month
  • Yearly: $4.99/year (includes 7-day free trial)

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings. We do not have access to your payment information — all transactions are handled by Apple.

6. Third-party services

StreakGrid does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

7. Children's privacy

StreakGrid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at samuel@samuelbrask.com.

8. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling StreakGrid.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
